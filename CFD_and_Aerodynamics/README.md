# CFD & Aerodynamics

Projects spanning open-source solvers (OpenFOAM, T-Flows), commercial tools (ANSYS Fluent), and low-order methods (XFLR5), progressing from fundamental flow simulations to specialised applications such as Lagrangian particle tracking and chaos theory.

Developed through coursework at EPFL and HKUST, and a year-long CFD mentorship with [Mohamed Aly Sayed](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mohamedsayedh/) ([CFD Dose](https://www.cfddose.com/)).

---

| Project | Description | Tools |
|---|---|---|
| [Particle Deposition in a 90° Bend](./Particle_Deposition_Prediction_in_a_90-Degree_Bend) | Lagrangian particle tracking and deposition prediction in turbulent pipe flow with custom user modules | T-Flows (Fortran) |
| [Airfoil Ground Effect](./Airfoil_Ground_Effect_Simulation) | Aerodynamic analysis of a NACA airfoil operating in proximity to the ground | ANSYS Fluent |
| [2D Cylinder & NACA Airfoil](./Flow_Simulation_2D__Cylinder_and_NACA_Airfoil_Analysis) | Steady and unsteady external flow simulations: vortex shedding, lift/drag characterisation | OpenFOAM |
| [Turbulence & The Skeleton of Chaos](./Turbulence_Finite_Reynolds_Chaos) | K41 turbulence theory, experimental data post-processing, Lyapunov exponents and chaos theory | MATLAB |
| [Fixed-Wing Weather Drone](./Aerodynamics_of_a_fixed_weather_drone) | Wing and airfoil performance analysis combining analytical, numerical, and experimental methods | XFLR5 |
