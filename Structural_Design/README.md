Mechanical design and structural engineering work, centred on my contribution to the EPFL Rocket Team's 2023 competition vehicle.

## Anti-Buckling Ring — EPFL Rocket Team

**Role:** Structural Engineer (2022–2023)

Design, optimisation, and simulation of the anti-buckling ring for the internal structure of the ERT 2023 competition rocket, flown at the EUROC 2023 competition in Portugal.

### Scope

- **Design and CAD** — full part design in SolidWorks with 2D manufacturing drawings and 3D assemblies (included in this folder)
- **Structural analysis** — buckling load cases and structural verification of the ring under flight loads
- **Integration** — coordination with 4 subsystem teams (propulsion, avionics, recovery, payload) within a 15-engineer structures group
- **Manufacturing** — participation in the 6-month manufacturing campaign leading to the competition
