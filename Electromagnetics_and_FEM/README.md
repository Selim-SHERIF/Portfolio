# Electromagnetics & FEM Simulation

This domain covers my primary area of specialisation: finite element modelling of electromagnetic systems, with a focus on magnetic levitation for high-speed transportation.

The work in this area is carried out in the context of my Master thesis at EPFL in collaboration with **Swisspod Technologies**, on the modelling and design of an electromagnetic levitation and guidance system for a hyperloop vehicle.

---

## Scope of Work

- **3D FEM simulation of moving magnetic sources over ferromagnetic rails** — moving Halbach arrays and electromagnets, eddy current induction, and velocity-dependent force characterisation (COMSOL Multiphysics)
- **Nonlinear magnetic material modelling** — B-H curve saturation effects, skin depth resolution, and mesh convergence strategies for convection-dominated magnetic diffusion problems
- **Hybrid electromagnetic suspension design** — combined permanent magnet biasing with actively controlled electromagnets for levitation and guidance
- **Multi-fidelity modelling** — complementing FEM with equivalent circuit and analytical models for rapid design iteration of Linear Induction Motor systems
